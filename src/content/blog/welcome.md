# Welcome to the New Otago Polyfest Website

Welcome to the new home of Otago Polyfest!

<p><img src="/src/assets/images/photos/venue-crowd.jpg" alt="Audience haka tautoko" loading="lazy" title="Audience haka tautoko" /></p>

We're excited to launch our new website as we begin another incredible festival journey. Whether you're a performer, kaiako, tutor, whānau member, volunteer, sponsor, stallholder or first-time visitor, this website has been designed to make it easier than ever to stay connected with everything happening at Otago Polyfest.

Over the coming weeks, this site, alongside our social media platforms, will become your go-to place for festival news, important dates, registration information, programmes, resources and event updates. As planning continues, we'll regularly add new content, answer frequently asked questions and share stories from across our Polyfest community.

Otago Polyfest is much more than a week of performances. It is a celebration of culture, language, identity and community, bringing together thousands of young people from the Deep South to learn, perform and proudly share who they are. Every year, our festival is made possible through the support of ECE, schools, whānau, volunteers, partners and sponsors—it truly takes a village.

We're looking forward to sharing the journey with you as we count down to the festival. Be sure to check back regularly, follow us on social media, and subscribe to our newsletter so you don't miss any important announcements.

Thank you for being part of the Otago Polyfest whānau. We can't wait to celebrate with you soon.

**Nau mai, haere mai. Welcome to Otago Polyfest.**
